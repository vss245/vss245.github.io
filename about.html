about me





Hi, I’m Veronika. I’d like to call myself a cognitive neuroscientist, but I might be under-qualified for that description for now. I have a BSc in Neuroscience from NYU (with a minor in Computer Science) and a MSc in Neural Systems and Computation from the University of Zürich and ETH Zürich. I’m interested in brain oscillations, cognition, memory, mental disorders and other things. I’m currently at Charité Berlin working on functional connectivity in EEG/MEG.
